Tonkotsu Ramen

Ingredients

Tonkotsu Stock

  • 1.25kg pork backbones
  • 1.25kg pork knuckle bones
  • 50g Kombu
  • 5 litres of water (reduced to 2 litres of stock)

Tonkotsu Ramen Soup

  • 600ml Tonkotsu stock
  • 50g soy sauce
  • 10g Mirin
  • 2g salt
  • 2 clove garlic, minced

Pork Char Shu

  • 500g rolled pork belly
  • 1litre of water
  • 400ml of the pork belly cooking in water
  • 60g sugar
  • 50g Mirin
  • 250g soy sauce
  • 15g minced garlic
  • 8g fresh ginger, sliced
  • 1 chilli, finely sliced

Aroma Oil

  • 280g pork back fat
  • 5g minced garlic
  • 15g minced pork

Toppings

  • 2 Eggs
  • Chilli oil
  • Spring onions
