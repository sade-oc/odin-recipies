Chicken Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1/2 onion sliced
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp MSG
  • 1/2 packet of beansprouts
  • 800g egg noodles
  • Dark Soy sauce
  • A little Oil

Method

1. Poach the chicken breasts in s little chicken stock. When cooled slice them.

2. Add a little oil to a HOT wok.

3. Add in the onion and cook for 1 minutes.

4. Add in the slices cooked chicken and heat up for 2-3 minutes.

5. Add in the salt and MSG. Stir for 30 seconds

6. Now add the noodles and beansprouts,toss to combine . When warmed add the Dark soy , toss to coat. Serve topped with sliced spring onions.
